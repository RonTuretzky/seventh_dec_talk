8 Million Empty Houses

That's a real number. 8 million abandoned "akiya" houses. Many are free or under $10k.

  • Villages are literally recruiting people to move there
  • Akiya Collective is already doing this (go check them out: akiyacollective.org)
  • This is not a scam, it's demographics

Also this is the OPPOSITE of tech bro "network state" stuff:

  • No gatekeeping by wealth
  • No VCs trying to flip land
  • Actually helping revitalize communities, not colonizing them

Crypto, But Make It Actually Useful

Look, I know. Crypto has... a reputation. But hear me out.

Forget speculation. Cryptography is actually good for:

  • Privacy infrastructure so corporations can't datamine your commune
  • Coordination tools for managing shared resources without a bank
  • Collective decision-making that actually scales

We're not trying to get rich. We're trying to coordinate without landlords.
